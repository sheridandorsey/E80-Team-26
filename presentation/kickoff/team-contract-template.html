Mission

Note

Team members share truthfully what they want from their E80 team experience. Identify and describe your team’s mission which will serve you as a guide for setting priorities, making decisions and how members will work together.

We agree that the purpose of our team is…

Example from an E4 team

The purpose of our team is to use the process of creating a successful composting design as an avenue to develop both our engineering and interpersonal skills. We strive to maintain a positive team dynamic by allocating tasks fairly and communicating both regularly and effectively.

Logistics / Organization

Organization Information to Include

Describe in detail how you will:

  • Communicate with each other
    • Medium (e.g., email, text, Discord)
    • Frequency
    • Expected response time
  • Store, update, and exchange information (Google Drive, Discord, Slack, etc.)
  • Hold effective meetings (Schedule, Agenda, Roles, Meeting Minutes, etc.)
  • Track task progress & completion (who will do what by when?)

Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths & Weaknesses

List here each team member’s strengths (e.g., strong Python skills, detail oriented, inclusive, focus on excellence, doer attitude, loves research, positive attitude, etc.) and weaknesses (tends to speak a lot, tends to speak very little, no CAD experience, tends to be late for meetings, etc.), how each team member plans to apply their strengths to the team’s success, and how each team member plans to work on improving/compensating their weaknesses.

Objectives

Example Team Objectives

Describe in detail your team’s objectives:

  • Grades
  • Quality of work
  • Desired participation of team members and how will you measure it
  • Distribution of work
  • Development of specific technical skills
  • Development of teamwork skills

Team Norms

Example Team Norms

Describe in detail your team’s rules of engagement:

  • How the team will create and maintain psychological safety
  • How you will ensure to create a healthy feedback culture
  • How your team makes decisions
  • How your team handles & resolves conflicts
  • How you hold each other accountable
  • The consequences for breaking rules
  • Anything else important for your team (e.g., humor)

Team Signatures

  1. I participated in formulating the mission, logistics, strengths and weaknesses, objectives, and ground rules as stated in this contract.
  2. I understand that I am obligated to abide by these terms and conditions.
  3. I understand that if I do not abide by these terms and conditions, I will suffer the consequences as stated in this contract.
